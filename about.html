About ADENTITI

Cybersecurity is not just technical. It is human.

ADENTITI Limited is a Canadian cybersecurity company focused on protecting people, businesses, and communities in an increasingly complex digital world.

We go beyond traditional cybersecurity by combining technology, education, storytelling, and human-centred design to help individuals and organizations understand threats, reduce risk, and build long-term digital resilience.

Because today, cybersecurity isn’t just a system problem — it’s a human one.

Why ADENTITI Was Formed

People are often the most targeted — and the least protected — part of cybersecurity.

Small businesses, families, seniors, and young people are often left behind by complex, technical solutions. ADENTITI exists to bridge that gap.

We simplify cybersecurity. We humanize it. We make it accessible — so people can recognize threats before they happen and respond with confidence.

Founder: Makafui Bokor

Building a human-centered future in cybersecurity.

Makafui Bokor is the founder of ADENTITI — a cybersecurity company focused on protecting businesses, families, and communities through practical, human-centered solutions.

With over a decade of experience in website design, cybersecurity awareness, and IT systems, Makafui bridges the gap between technical security and real-world understanding — making cybersecurity not just a service, but an experience people can relate to.

Beyond traditional services, he creates immersive learning through books, film, speaking engagements, and interactive tools — helping people recognize threats, build resilience, and respond with confidence.
