About ADENTITI

Cybersecurity is not just technical. It is human.

ADENTITI Limited is a Canadian cybersecurity company focused on protecting people, businesses, and communities in an increasingly complex digital world.

We go beyond traditional cybersecurity by combining technology, education, storytelling, and human-centered design to help individuals and organizations understand threats, reduce risk, and build long-term digital resilience.

Because today, cybersecurity isn’t just a system problem — it’s a human one.

Why ADENTITI Was Formed

People are the most targeted — and the least protected — part of cybersecurity.

Small businesses, families, seniors, and young people are often left behind by complex, technical solutions. ADENTITI exists to bridge that gap.

We simplify cybersecurity. We humanize it. We make it accessible — so people can recognize threats before they happen and respond with confidence.

Founder: Makafui Bokor

Makafui Bokor is the founder of ADENTITI, dedicated to helping businesses, professionals, and communities navigate digital risks.

With over a decade of experience in website design, cybersecurity awareness, and IT solutions, he focuses on making cybersecurity practical, understandable, and accessible.
