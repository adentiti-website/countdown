Case Study: Securing a Healthcare Provider

A leading healthcare provider in Toronto with over 500 employees was grappling with frequent phishing attacks and data breaches. The organization needed a robust solution to protect sensitive patient information and maintain compliance with healthcare regulations.

What ADENTITI Did:

  • Conducted a comprehensive security audit to identify vulnerabilities in their network infrastructure.
  • Deployed cutting-edge firewalls, endpoint protection, and an intrusion detection system.
  • Provided gamified cybersecurity training to educate employees on identifying phishing emails and other common threats.
  • Implemented a secure backup system to ensure data recovery in the event of an attack.
  • Set up 24/7 monitoring and real-time threat response protocols.

Outcome: Within six months, the healthcare provider experienced a 70% reduction in phishing incidents, zero data breaches, and improved employee confidence in handling cyber threats.
